# FGA (Algebraic Grammar)

**A structured linguistic framework for reliable critical reasoning and analysis.**
FGA transforms natural language into precise algebraic representations with strong emphasis on **core-idea preservation**, fact/opinion classification, premise validation, modifier tracking, and high-credibility scoring.
## Why FGA Matters
In an era dominated by LLMs that often paraphrase or dilute meaning, FGA is designed for **mechanical fidelity** and trustworthy logical transparency. It excels at handling complex, nested texts while preserving the author's original intent — making it especially valuable for founders, researchers, and investors who need reliable analysis of proposals, frameworks, and philosophical material.
## Key Capabilities
- Binary Subject Brick vs Predicate/Payload split on every clause
- Robust Fact vs Opinion classification (including analytical opinions)
- Premise chain validation with relevance, sufficiency, and violation detection
- Modifier (Cement) tracking with explicit subject binding
- "the + adjective" nominalized noun detection
- Parallelism and subject continuity checks
- Algebraic representation ready for advanced reasoning
- Capitalization / credibility scoring for investor materials
